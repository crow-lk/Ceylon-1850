Discover Our Packages

Choose the experience that fits your day. Village Explorer (Half-Day) includes a traditional welcome, craft demos, and hands-on village-kitchen skills, followed by a garden-to-kitchen lunch. Family Day Out (Full-Day) adds sweet-making and extra time for photos and stories at a relaxed pace—perfect for families with kids. Both options are small-group, hosted by our local family in Udawalawe. .

Looking for something specific? Heritage Workshop (Custom) is tailored for schools, universities, and teams—focus on cooking, crafts, or cultural talks, with flexible timing. All packages include guided activities, ingredients, drinking water, and safety briefings. Start times: morning 9:00 or afternoon 14:00. We’re 1.4 km from the Elephant Orphanage; pricing is available on request or via seasonal promotions.
