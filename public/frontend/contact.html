Ceylon 1850 brings Sri Lanka’s living heritage to life in Udawalawe. Spend a half or full day with local hosts—try gem mining, cinnamon stick making, coconut crafts, paddy-husking and traditional sweet making—then share a garden-to-kitchen meal. We keep groups small, respect the community and environment, and welcome families and travellers of all ages.
