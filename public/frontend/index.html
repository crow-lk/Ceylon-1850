Living Heritage

Hands-on Village Experience

Step into living heritage at Ceylon 1850 in Udawalawe — a hands-on village experience crafted by local families. From your cinnamon-tea welcome to the stories behind our crafts and cuisine, every moment is authentic, relaxed, and memorable.

Pan for gems, roll cinnamon sticks, weave with coconut leaves, try paddy-husking and winnowing, then harvest fresh vegetables to cook a garden-to-kitchen lunch. Finish with Sri Lankan sweets like kokis and kavum. Just 1.4 km south of the Elephant Orphanage, it’s the perfect complement to your safari day.

Traditional Welcome

Slip into village style, sip cinnamon tea or king coconut, and learn how we greet guests in the old way—slow, warm, and personal.
